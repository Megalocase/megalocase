Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 September 2025

1. Purpose of the Application

This application, Redbubble Poster, is used to automate the creation and publishing of Pins on Pinterest boards. It retrieves product information (title, description, hashtags, and image) from Google Sheets and uses Pinterest API to publish Pins. The purpose is solely for marketing and promotion of Redbubble product designs.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The application only uses information provided in Google Sheets and authorization tokens obtained from Pinterest API.

3. Use of Data

Authorization tokens obtained from Pinterest are used exclusively for connecting to Pinterest API and publishing content. No personal data is stored or transmitted to third parties.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer user information to outside parties. The application communicates only with Pinterest API in order to perform its publishing functions.

5. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there are no user accounts or data to modify or delete. If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at the email below.
